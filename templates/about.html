01. NLP Core

Built on spaCy, our engine utilizes specialized Named Entity Recognition models to distinguish between professional experiences, academic degrees, and individual identifiers with surgical accuracy.

02. Vector Match

Processes textual data into high-dimensional vectors. Matches are calculated using TF-IDF and Cosine Similarity to ensure that candidates aren't just matching keywords, but semantic intent.

03. High Throughput

Optimized Flask architecture allows for parallel processing of massive resume datasets, providing real-time matrix comparisons for recruitment mandates.
